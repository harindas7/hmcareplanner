App Features

Everything Your Carers Need — In Their Pocket

The HMCarePlanner mobile app gives carers real-time access to their schedules, client details, and medication information.

View Schedules

Carers can view their daily and weekly schedules with all visit details, client information, and care notes.

GPS Check-In/Out

One-tap check-in and check-out with automatic GPS location recording for full visit verification.

Medication Updates

Record medication administration in real time. View medication schedules and confirm doses directly from the app.

Update Visit Notes

Add care notes, record observations, and update service details while at the client's location.

Push Notifications

Receive instant alerts for schedule changes, new assignments, and important updates.

Secure & Private

Secure login with role-based access. Client data is encrypted and GDPR compliant at all times.
