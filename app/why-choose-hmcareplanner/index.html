Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How is HMCarePlanner different from OnCare, Birdie, or Log my Care?

Unlike many competitors, HMCarePlanner uses fixed monthly pricing — not per-carer fees that escalate as you grow. We also include eMAR, GPS tracking, payroll reports, and invoicing in every plan as standard, where others charge extra for these modules.

Will my costs increase as I add more care workers?

No. Our plans are based on client numbers, not care worker numbers. Add as many staff as you need within your plan tier without paying more. This makes budgeting predictable and means your software cost doesn't spiral as you recruit.

How long does it take to set up HMCarePlanner?

Most agencies are up and running within a few days. You can create your account in under 2 minutes, and our team provides free onboarding support and training to get your clients, staff, and schedules loaded quickly.

Can I migrate data from my current care software?

Yes. Our support team can help you migrate client records, staff profiles, and care plans from your existing system. We'll guide you through the process step by step to ensure nothing is lost.

Is my data secure and GDPR compliant?

Absolutely. HMCarePlanner uses enterprise-grade encryption and UK-based cloud hosting. We are fully GDPR compliant, with role-based access controls ensuring only authorised staff can view sensitive client information.

What support do you offer?

Every plan includes free UK-based support, free training for your team, and free onboarding assistance. There are no premium support tiers — every customer gets the same level of dedicated help.
