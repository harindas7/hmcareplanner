Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

How much does HMCarePlanner cost?

Plans start from £59.99 per month for the Starter plan (up to 25 clients). The Business plan is £99.99/mo (up to 75 clients) and the Premium plan is £149.99/mo (unlimited clients). All plans include core features with no hidden fees.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a full 14-day free trial with access to all features. No credit card or payment details required. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time, and all your data carries over.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle, and all your data is preserved when switching plans.

Is eMAR included in all plans?

Yes, our eMAR (Electronic Medication Administration Records) module is included in every plan at no extra cost. Many competitors charge extra for medication management — we include it as standard.

Are there any long-term contracts?

No. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel at any time. We believe in earning your business every month.

Is HMCarePlanner CQC compliant?

Yes. HMCarePlanner is designed with CQC compliance in mind. It generates audit-ready reports, maintains complete care records, and provides the evidence trail inspectors require. Our structured care plans and digital records help agencies meet all key lines of enquiry.
