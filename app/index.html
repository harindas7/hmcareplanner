The Biggest Challenges UK Care Agencies Face — And How We Fix Them

74% of UK home care agencies cite staff shortages, compliance burden, and operational chaos as their biggest barriers. HMCarePlanner was built to solve exactly these problems.

Problem

CQC Compliance Is Overwhelming

Care agencies struggle to keep up with changing CQC regulations. Paper-based records, missing documentation, and incomplete audit trails put agencies at risk of poor inspection ratings.

HMCarePlanner Solution

Automatic digital audit trails, structured care plans, and CQC-ready reports — always inspection-ready without the admin headache.

Problem

Scheduling & Rostering Is Chaotic

Manual scheduling via spreadsheets leads to double-bookings, missed visits, and wasted hours. 74% of agencies say staff shortages make this even harder — rotas constantly need reshuffling.

HMCarePlanner Solution

Smart scheduling with automatic repeat visits, staff availability tracking, and real-time rota management — saving hours every week.

Problem

Paper MAR Charts Risk Medication Errors

Paper-based medication records are prone to errors, difficult to audit, and offer no real-time visibility. Missed or wrong doses put vulnerable clients at serious risk.

HMCarePlanner Solution

Digital eMAR with automated reminders, real-time dose tracking, and a full audit trail — eliminating paper errors and ensuring patient safety.

Problem

High Staff Turnover & Carer Burnout

98% of UK care agencies are actively recruiting. Long hours, excessive admin, and inefficient processes lead to burnout — causing carers to leave for better-paid roles in other sectors.

HMCarePlanner Solution

Our mobile app and automated workflows reduce admin burden dramatically. Carers spend less time on paperwork and more time caring — improving job satisfaction and retention.

Problem

No Real-Time Visibility of Care Delivery

Without live tracking, managers can't tell if visits are happening on time. Missed visits go undetected, care quality drops, and families lose trust in the agency.

HMCarePlanner Solution

GPS check-in/check-out, live dashboard, and instant missed-visit alerts give you complete visibility — know exactly what's happening, in real time.

Problem

Payroll & Invoicing Eats Up Hours

63% of carers are only paid for time in the client's home. Manually calculating hours, travel pay, and generating invoices is slow, error-prone, and leads to payment disputes.

HMCarePlanner Solution

Automatic payroll calculations from completed visits, travel cost tracking, and one-click invoice generation — accurate payments, every time.
