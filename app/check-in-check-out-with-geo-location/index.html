Check-In & Check-Out With Geo Location Detection

HMCarePlanner call monitoring module enables your home care agency to record visit times and locations. Carers use the mobile app to check-in, check-out, and update details.

Live Tracking

Know Where Your Carers Are — In Real Time

Full visibility into every visit. GPS-verified check-ins and check-outs give you confidence that the right carer is at the right place at the right time.

GPS-Verified Location

Each check-in and check-out records the carer's GPS coordinates, confirming they are at the client's location.

Accurate Time Logging

Precise arrival and departure times are automatically recorded — no more manual timesheets or guesswork.

Missed Visit Alerts

Get notified immediately if a carer hasn't checked in within the expected window. Take action before issues arise.

Visit Reports

Generate detailed reports on visit compliance, durations, and locations for management review and CQC evidence.
